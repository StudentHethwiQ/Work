    Problem & Solution

    The original design of the Gemini app, when compared to its Apple counterpart—Siri, lacks the refined transparency, sleek aesthetics, and modern fluidity that define a polished user experience. It feels unfinished, lacking the finesse that makes an interface truly immersive. My goal? To elevate it. I aimed to infuse a polished, modern feel—one that’s both inviting and sophisticated. By blending a futuristic, glass-like aesthetic with organic, fluid design elements, I crafted a UI that’s not just visually striking but also effortlessly intuitive.
